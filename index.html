Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/8: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

High-Precision Extrusion

Our advanced extruders melt and form PE compounds at precise temperatures ensuring uniform flow and consistent output quality.

  • Controlled temperature zones
  • Precision-designed die heads

Controlled Cooling & Vacuum Sizing

Extruded pipes pass through vacuum sizing tanks and water-cooling baths to achieve perfect dimensional accuracy and structural integrity.

  • Rapid cooling for structural integrity
  • Vacuum sizing for dimensional accuracy

Precise Dimensional Control

Automated measurement systems continuously monitor OD, wall thickness and ovality to ensure every pipe meets exact specifications.

  • Outer diameter tolerance ±0.1mm
  • Wall thickness uniformity assured

Rigorous Quality Inspection

Every pipe undergoes pressure testing, visual inspection and dimensional verification against IS 5984 and ISO 4427 standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Visual & dimensional inspection

Product Identification & Marking

Inkjet printing systems apply permanent markings including batch code, date of manufacture, pressure rating and certification marks.

  • Batch & date coding
  • Specification & cert marking

Precision Cutting to Length

Automated saw cutters produce clean, square, burr-free pipe ends at exact lengths ready for installation or coiling.

  • Automatic length cutting
  • Clean, burr-free square ends

Safe & Efficient Packaging

Pipes are bundled, banded and labelled for safe transit. Coiled pipes are wound on drums and shrink-wrapped for weather protection.

  • Bundle strapping & labelling
  • Weatherproof coil packaging
